About me

My fascination with robotics started early — I was the kid who took apart every toy to understand what was inside. That curiosity still drives me today as I complete my B.Eng. in Robotics Engineering at Universite de Sherbrooke.

I build real-world autonomous systems across robotics internships and personal ventures, with practical experience in ROS2, Linux, embedded deployment on Jetson and Raspberry Pi, and applied perception using OpenCV and PyTorch.
